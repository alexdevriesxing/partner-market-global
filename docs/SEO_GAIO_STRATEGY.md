# Partner Market Global SEO / GAIO Strategy

Current canonical domain: `https://partner-market-global2.pages.dev`

Future custom domain target: `https://www.partnermarketglobal.com`

## Core positioning

Partner Market Global is a curated B2B showcase for import, export, distribution, licensing, private label, OEM and franchise opportunities. The site should rank and be cited for search and AI-answer queries such as:

- "import opportunities for distributors"
- "export ready brands looking for importers"
- "franchise opportunities by country"
- "master franchise rights available"
- "distribution rights opportunities"
- "private label manufacturers looking for partners"
- "B2B international business partner marketplace"

## Technical SEO included

- Next.js metadata per page
- Canonical URLs for all important pages
- Open Graph and Twitter card metadata
- `robots.ts`, static `robots.txt` and `sitemap.ts`
- Organization and WebSite JSON-LD
- Offer JSON-LD on opportunity pages
- FAQ JSON-LD on homepage
- Semantic headings and descriptive alt text
- Local optimized WebP/PNG/SVG assets
- Long-form detail page structure for entity clarity
- `llms.txt` for non-Google AI/answer-engine context

## GAIO / AEO recommendations

1. Add direct-answer sections to category pages:
   - "What is an import opportunity?"
   - "How do distribution rights work?"
   - "What is a master franchise?"
   - "How do companies list an opportunity?"

2. Build pages for high-intent categories:
   - `/import-opportunities`
   - `/export-opportunities`
   - `/franchise-opportunities`
   - `/distribution-rights`
   - `/master-franchise-opportunities`
   - `/private-label-oem-opportunities`
   - `/country-partner-opportunities`

3. Build location pages:
   - `/import-opportunities-indonesia`
   - `/distribution-rights-europe`
   - `/franchise-opportunities-asean`
   - `/export-opportunities-japan`
   - `/b2b-partners-middle-east`

4. Keep every opportunity profile structured:
   - opportunity type
   - sector
   - origin country
   - target markets
   - ideal partner profile
   - commercial model
   - risk notes
   - documents available
   - inquiry CTA

5. Add editorial content:
   - "How to evaluate an import opportunity"
   - "Distributor agreement checklist"
   - "Master franchise vs area development rights"
   - "How to find qualified international distributors"
   - "What importers look for in export-ready brands"

## Trust and compliance language

The website should always make clear that Partner Market Global curates and showcases business opportunities but does not provide legal, financial, investment, franchise, tax or securities advice. Each party must conduct its own due diligence.
