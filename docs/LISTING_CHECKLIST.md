# Listing Checklist

Before approving a listing, collect and review:

## Business identity

- Company legal name
- Registration number
- Website and official email domain
- Primary contact person
- Country of incorporation
- Operating address where relevant

## Opportunity clarity

- Opportunity type
- Target markets
- Partner type required
- Territory availability
- Commercial model
- Investment or working capital expectation
- Exclusivity conditions
- Minimum requirements

## Credentials

- Product catalogue
- Company profile
- Certifications or licenses where relevant
- Franchise deck where relevant
- Brand guidelines or marketing assets
- Export documents where relevant

## Risk notes

- Regulatory requirements
- Product registration needs
- Franchise disclosure requirements
- Labelling requirements
- Import restrictions
- Working capital risks
- Operational capacity requirements

## Publishing

- Hero image
- Short card summary
- Full profile copy
- CTA and inquiry form
- Verification badges
- SEO title and description
- JSON-LD checked
